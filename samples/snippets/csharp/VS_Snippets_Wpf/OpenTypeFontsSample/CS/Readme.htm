The Sample OpenType Font Pack fonts have limited character sets and each demonstrates specific OpenType features, including small caps, ligatures, old style figures, swashes and alternates. The OpenType features of each Sample OpenType Font Pack font are listed below:

·         Kootenay - proportional figures, tabular figures and old style figures

·         Lindsey - alternative vowels {2 additional of each a,e,i,o,u and y}, ligatures {bb, cc, dd, ee, ff, gg, ll, mm, nn, oo, pp, rr, ss and tt}, proportional figures and tabular figures

·         Miramonte Regular – proportional figures, tabular figures and alternative lowercase {a and ae}

·         Miramonte Bold –proportional figures, tabular figures and alternative lowercase {a and ae}

·         Pericles Light – alternative inscriptional forms {E,O and R}, alternative narrow forms {C,G and S}, and alternative ligature forms {TH and HE}

·         Pericles Regular – alternative inscriptional forms {E,O and R}, alternative narrow forms {C,G and S}, and alternative ligature forms {TH and HE})

·         Pescadero Roman – proportional figures, tabular figures, old style figures and initial swash caps

·         Pescadero Bold – proportional figures, tabular figures, old style figures and initial swash caps
